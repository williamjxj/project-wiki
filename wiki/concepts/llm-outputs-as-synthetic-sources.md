# LLM Outputs as Synthetic Sources

## Consensus

ChatGPT frames LLM chat exports as first-pass synthetic knowledge, not ground truth — 80% is repetitive and differently phrased. The wiki distillation layer is where signal emerges from noise. This epistemic reframe is foundational: LLMs produce opinions and suggestions that must be deduplicated, distilled, and reconciled before they become actionable engineering knowledge. _(single source — ChatGPT)_
